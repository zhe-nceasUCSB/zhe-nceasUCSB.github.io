### Surface Urban Heat Islands 

![](/images/heat-island.png)

My research examines how land cover shapes urban heat islands in Boise, Phoenix, and Las Vegas using advanced spatial statistic models. I quantify vegetation and built-up areas, and apply GWR, STWR, and MGWR to analyze surface urban heat island patterns, biophysical indicators, and socioeconomic factors.

### Tickbase

![](/images/tickbase.png)

[Tickbase Project Website](https://tickbase.net)

As part of an NSF-funded project, I was the leader of the spatiotemporal modeling group and collaborated across disciplines to model tick-borne diseases using remote sensing data. My work processes 20 years of climate "big data," linking environmental factors to disease dynamics and developing a framework to manage large, complex ecological datasets in New England and specifically in Connecticut towns.
